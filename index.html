Logos for RDF Formats

These are icons for various RDF-related formats:
RDF/XML, Turtle, N-Triples, RDFa, SPARQL, R2RML and SHACL.

They are available in PNG and SVG versions.

These icons are adapted from
Bill Schwappacher's original RDF logo
by Richard Cyganiak.
