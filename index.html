Icons for RDF Formats

These are logos for various RDF formats:
RDF/XML, Turtle, N-Triples, RDFa, and SPARQL.
They are available in PNG and SVG versions.

These icons were made by Richard Cyganiak
based on Bill Schwappacher's original RDF logo.
